Frequently Asked Questions

Do you haul away junk or donate items? ▼

No—we focus on sorting, organizing, and preparing items for removal or donation. We stage everything so junk haulers and donation centers can work efficiently. This approach keeps our process fast and focused, and it actually makes removal services cheaper and faster for you.

How long does a reset actually take? ▼

A typical reset takes one focused day (usually 4-8 hours depending on the space size). We work efficiently to complete meaningful progress in one visit, so you don't have weeks of disruption.

What if my space is really large? ▼

We can break larger projects into multiple focused resets, each addressing one specific zone or category. This keeps momentum going and prevents decision fatigue. Contact us to discuss your space.

Do you work with businesses too? ▼

Absolutely! We help businesses organize warehouses, inventory spaces, offices, and storage areas to improve efficiency and operations. The same flat-rate, one-day reset approach works for commercial spaces.

What areas do you serve? ▼

We currently serve the Pittsburgh and South Hills area. If you're in the region and have a space that needs resetting, contact us for availability.
